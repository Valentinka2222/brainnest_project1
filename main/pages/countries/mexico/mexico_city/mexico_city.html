Mexico City

Architectural Landmark

The vibrant capital is an entire state to discover. The Historic center of Mexico City has an abundance of well preserved historic architecture, which, along with Xochimilco’s floating gardens, is protected by UNESCO. Visitors are welcome to discover the local cuisine in numerous restaurants, art in the museums and hip urban life, which is busy all day and all night. Why to go there? One of the most important cities in Americas – Mexico City is definitely a place to see at least once in your lifetime!
